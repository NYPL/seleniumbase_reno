On the third floor, you'll discover our outside terrace and the Bronx's largest collection of adult circulating fiction and nonfiction books. This diverse selection of materials includes the circulating Bronx Collection which features books about the Bronx and works by Bronx authors, celebrating the rich culture of our borough. It also includes the Educator Collection, which provides valuable resources for educators, students, and school communities.

Additionally, you'll find popular magazines and newspapers, as well as large print materials and audiobooks, ensuring that there's something for everyone to enjoy!

Available programs and services include:

  • Social Work Intern
  • Book Discussions & Author Talks 
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Adult Gaming 
  • Creative Writing
  • Trivia and other various programing for our Adult and Emerging Adult Population  

 

Please note that seating on the terrace is first-come, first-served, and we cannot guarantee availability for individuals or groups.
