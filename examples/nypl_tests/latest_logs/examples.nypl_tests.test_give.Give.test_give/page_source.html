Give

Government funding pays only a portion of the Library's operating expenses. We depend on support from generous individuals like you to help keep our books, classes, and resources FREE for all New Yorkers.

Help add 17,000 new books to our shelves!

We're raising $350,000 to buy new books, all of which will be available for free to millions of New Yorkers. But replenishing our collections is expensive: the average cost of a new bookphysical or electronicis $20. Donate to the NYPL Book Fund and your gift will be doubled by a generous donor.

Get Involved

  • Planned Giving

    Leave a legacy by supporting the Library via bequests, charitable IRA distributions, gifts from your estate and will, and more. Provide free and open access to education and opportunity for generations to come.

  • Benefit Events

    Show your support for the Library by attending some of New York's most exclusive events, including the Library Lions Gala, Library Lunch, Young Lions Fiction Awards, and more.

More Ways to Give

  • Honor & Memorial Gifts

    Remember or celebrate someone special by making a gift in a loved one's name.

  • Book Dedication

    Print a name and personalized message inside a New York Public Library circulating book.

  • Chair Dedication

    Personalize a chair plaque in our iconic Rose Main Reading Room or newly renovated Stavros Niarchos Foundation Library.

  • Donor-Advised Funds

    Select the Library for a fully tax-deductible grant from your Donor Advised Fund and support millions of New Yorkers.

  • Start Your Own Campaign

    Dedicate your birthday, celebrate your wedding, honor a loved one, or create your own campaign in support of the Library.
